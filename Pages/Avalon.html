Building System

In the building system, I implemented the rotation of the 3D volume selection.

Although it may sound simple, the selection volume was not just a basic box with a shader applied. It is dynamically generated at runtime to support advanced gameplay features.

To enable proper rotation, I implemented the solution using matrix rotation mathematics, applying custom transformation logic to ensure correct orientation and behavior within the system.

This required a solid understanding of 3D math and transformation matrices.

Combat Behavior

Within the combat behavior system, I implemented a strategic ranged attack feature for certain melee NPCs.

This behavior triggers when:

- The NPC has a high-ground advantage, or
- The straight-line distance to the player is shorter than the calculated pathfinding distance.

This allowed melee enemies to behave more intelligently and strategically depending on terrain and navigation constraints, improving combat dynamics and realism.

Bug Fixes & System Improvements

I worked on bug fixes across multiple areas of the game, including gameplay systems, animation, and AI.

In the animation system, I fixed logic issues between the Animation Instance and the Behavior Tree that were causing NPCs to slide under certain conditions.

I also corrected blending issues between movement and attack animations, ensuring proper transitions and preventing incorrect animation calls.
